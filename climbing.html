I climb.
I shoot.
I get it.

I'm a high-level climber based in Bangkok, deeply embedded in the local gym scene and outdoor climbing community. When I pick up a camera at a climbing event or shoot, I already know where the crux is, what the send looks like, and which moment will matter.

When Stonegoat Climbing opened in Bangkok, I was brought on as their first and sole content creator. The work we built together helped ignite a new era of Thai climbing culture. Other gyms took notice, raised their standards, and the community grew around it. That's not something I take lightly.

From bouldering competitions to sport climbing in the limestone crags of Thailand, I handle everything solo. Shoot, edit, deliver.
